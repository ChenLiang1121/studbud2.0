Welcome to the home page

This is an online web application which helps you to manage
your writing tasks at school or work.

Use navigation bar to shift between webpage tools.


"Tasks" is for adding and clarifying task details.

"Kanban board" is for putting tasks into stage groups.

"Timer" is for managing your time of study.

"Dictionary" is for searching word's definition and synonyms.
